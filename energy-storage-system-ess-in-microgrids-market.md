<p><p>Energy Storage System (ESS) in Microgrids Market Types include Grid-Tied Type and Independent Type. Grid-Tied Type systems are connected to the main grid and store excess energy for later use or to sell back to the grid. Independent Type systems operate autonomously, disconnected from the main grid, providing reliable power to remote areas or critical facilities. Both types offer enhanced energy reliability, efficiency, and flexibility, making them essential components for modern microgrid applications.</p></p>
