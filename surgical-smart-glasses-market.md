<p><p>Surgical smart glasses are available in two main market types - wire type and wireless type. Wire type surgical smart glasses are connected to a power source or device through a physical wire, providing consistent power and data transmission. On the other hand, wireless type surgical smart glasses operate without the need for a physical connection, using wireless technology such as Bluetooth or WiFi to transmit data. Both types of smart glasses offer hands-free operation and visual information display for surgeons during medical procedures.</p></p>
