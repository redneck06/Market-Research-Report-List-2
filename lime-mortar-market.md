<p><strong>The Lime Mortar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Lime Mortars</li><li>Non-hydraulic Lime Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lime mortar market consists of two main types: hydraulic lime mortars and non-hydraulic lime mortar. Hydraulic lime mortars are known for their ability to set and harden through a chemical reaction with water, providing higher strength and durability. On the other hand, non-hydraulic lime mortars rely on carbonation for setting and hardening, making them more breathable and ideal for historic buildings. Both types of lime mortars are popular choices in restoration and conservation projects due to their compatibility with traditional construction methods.</p></p>
