<p><ul><li>CVD</li><li>HPHT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Jewelry Synthetic Diamonds market consists of two main types: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and HPHT (High Pressure High Temperature) diamonds. CVD diamonds are created by growing a diamond crystal on a substrate using a gas-phase chemical process, resulting in high-quality gemstones. HPHT diamonds are produced by subjecting carbon to extreme heat and pressure, mimicking the natural diamond formation process. Both types are popular choices for jewelry due to their cost-effectiveness, brilliance, and durability.</p></p>
