<p><p>Calcium Hypochlorite for Water Treatment Market Types include the Calcium Process and the Sodium Process. In the Calcium Process, Calcium Hypochlorite is produced by reacting calcium hydroxide with chlorine gas, forming a stable and high concentration product. On the other hand, the Sodium Process involves using sodium hydroxide as the reactant, resulting in a slightly less concentrated product compared to the Calcium Process. Both processes are commonly used in water treatment plants to disinfect and purify water for various uses.</p></p>
