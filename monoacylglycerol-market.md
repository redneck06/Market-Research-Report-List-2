<p><p>Monoacylglycerol market can be categorized into two main types: General Monoacylglycerol and Distilled Monoacylglycerol. General Monoacylglycerol is produced through the esterification of glycerol with fatty acids, whereas Distilled Monoacylglycerol is further purified through a distillation process to remove impurities and increase its purity level. Both types of Monoacylglycerol are widely used in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics due to their emulsifying, stabilizing, and moisturizing properties.</p></p>
