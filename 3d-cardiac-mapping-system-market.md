<p><p>The 3D Cardiac Mapping System Market includes various types such as Electroanatomical Mapping, Basket Catheter Mapping, and Real-Time Positional Management (Cardiac Pathways) EP System. Electroanatomical mapping involves recording the electrical activity of the heart, while Basket Catheter Mapping uses a catheter with multiple electrodes to create 3D maps of the heart's electrical activity. Real-Time Positional Management EP Systems help guide catheter movements during procedures to treat cardiac conditions, providing accurate real-time feedback for precise navigation.</p></p>
