<p><p>Optical chips are categorized into two main types: Optical Active Chip and Optical Passive Chip. Optical Active Chips are integrated with active components like lasers or modulators, enabling them to actively manipulate light signals. On the other hand, Optical Passive Chips do not contain any active components and simply transmit or redirect light signals without altering them. Both types play a crucial role in various applications such as data communication, sensing, and imaging, driving the growth of the overall optical chip market.</p></p>
