<p><p>Structural glass market is segmented into two main types: frame type and frameless type. Frame type structural glass involves the use of supportive frames or fittings to hold the glass panels in place, providing structural support. On the other hand, frameless type structural glass uses engineered glass panels that are connected together without the need for visible frames, achieving a more seamless and modern aesthetic. Both types offer unique design possibilities and structural advantages for various architectural applications.</p></p>
