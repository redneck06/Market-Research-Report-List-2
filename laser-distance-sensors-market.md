<p><p>The laser distance sensors market can be segmented into various types based on technology, including digital laser sensors, CMOS laser sensors, and others. Digital laser sensors utilize advanced digital signal processing technology for accurate distance measurement. CMOS laser sensors combine CMOS image sensor technology with laser diodes for precise distance detection. Other types in the market may include Time-of-Flight (TOF) sensors, triangulation sensors, or laser radar systems. Each type offers unique features and benefits for different applications in industries such as manufacturing, robotics, and automation.</p></p>
