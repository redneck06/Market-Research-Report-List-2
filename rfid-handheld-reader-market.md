<p><p>RFID Handheld Readers are categorized into three main types based on the frequency they operate at: LF (Low Frequency), HF (High Frequency), and UHF (Ultra High Frequency). LF RFID Handheld Readers are commonly used for access control and animal tracking. HF RFID Handheld Readers are often used for payment transactions and inventory management. UHF RFID Handheld Readers are popular for supply chain management and asset tracking. Each market type serves specific needs and industries, providing various benefits in terms of range, speed, and compatibility.</p></p>
