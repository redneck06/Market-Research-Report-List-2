<p><p>Disposable slippers come in two main market types: closed-toe and open-toe. Closed-toe slippers provide full coverage for the foot, protecting it from dirt and germs. They are often preferred in healthcare settings or places where cleanliness is crucial. On the other hand, open-toe slippers have a design that exposes the toes, offering breathability and comfort. They are popular in spa and hotel settings where guests may want to easily slip them on and off. Both types cater to different preferences and purposes in the market.</p></p>
