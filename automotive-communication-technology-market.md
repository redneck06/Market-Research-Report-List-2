<p><p>Automotive Communication Technology refers to the various types of networks used in vehicles for communication between electronic control units. Local Interconnect Network (LIN) is typically used for low-speed communication, while Controller Area Network (CAN) is a standard for high-speed communication in vehicles. FlexRay is a high-speed communication protocol used for real-time data transmission. Media-Oriented Systems Transport (MOST) is commonly used for multimedia and infotainment systems. Ethernet is increasingly being adopted in vehicles for its high bandwidth capabilities and compatibility with other devices.</p></p>
