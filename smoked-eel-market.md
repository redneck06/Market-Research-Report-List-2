<p><p>Smoked eel can be found in two different market types: hot-smoke and cold-smoke. In hot-smoke markets, the eel is cooked at a higher temperature, resulting in a firmer texture and a stronger smoky flavor. Cold-smoke markets, on the other hand, involve smoking the eel at a lower temperature over a longer period of time, yielding a more delicate and subtle smoky taste. Both types of smoked eel offer unique flavor profiles and can be enjoyed in a variety of dishes.</p></p>
