<p><ul><li>Inrush Current Limitation</li><li>Antenna Matching</li><li>Snubbing Networks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic resistors are widely used in various applications such as inrush current limitation, antenna matching, and snubbing networks. Inrush current limitation involves using ceramic resistors to restrict the flow of current at the start-up of a device, preventing damage to components. Antenna matching utilizes ceramic resistors to ensure optimal transmission of radio frequency signals. Snubbing networks employ ceramic resistors to suppress voltage spikes and protect sensitive components in electronic circuits. These applications highlight the versatility and importance of ceramic resistors in various industries.</p></p>
