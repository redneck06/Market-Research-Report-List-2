<p><p>Endogenous Creatinine Assay Kits are used to measure the levels of creatinine in biological samples. There are two main types of assays used in the market - Jaffe's Kinetic Method and Enzymatic Method. The Jaffe's Kinetic Method relies on a colorimetric reaction to measure creatinine levels, while the Enzymatic Method uses specific enzymes to quantify creatinine. Both methods have their own advantages and limitations, and the choice between the two depends on the specific requirements of the assay and the sample being analyzed.</p></p>
