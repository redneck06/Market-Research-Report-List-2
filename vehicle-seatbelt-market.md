<p><p>Passive seatbelt systems rely solely on the occupant's motion to activate and securely fasten the belt in the event of a collision. These systems are typically found in older vehicles and are considered less effective than active seatbelt systems. Active seatbelt systems, on the other hand, use sensors and technology to automatically adjust the seatbelt and ensure it is properly secured at all times. This advanced technology provides a higher level of protection for occupants and is becoming increasingly popular in newer vehicles.</p></p>
