<p><p>LED Diffuse Reflective Photoelectric Sensors are available in two market types - Digital Signal and Analog Signal. Digital Signal sensors provide a binary output, typically in the form of on/off signals, making them suitable for applications that require simple detection. Analog Signal sensors, on the other hand, provide a continuous output proportional to the amount of light received, offering more precise detection capabilities. Both types of sensors are widely used in industrial automation and robotics for tasks such as object detection, distance measurement, and positioning.</p></p>
