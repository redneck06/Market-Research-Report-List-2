<p><strong>The Construction Glass Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Construction glass sealants are classified into two main types: single component and multi-component. Single-component sealants come ready for use straight out of the can, making them convenient and easy to apply. On the other hand, multi-component sealants require mixing of separate components before application, offering greater flexibility and control over curing time and adhesion strength. Each type of sealant has its own unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the construction project.</p></p>
