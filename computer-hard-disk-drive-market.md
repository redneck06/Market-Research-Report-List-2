<p><p>The computer hard disk drive market can be classified into two main types based on the bus interface used - IDE (Integrated Drive Electronics) Bus and SCSI Bus. IDE Bus drives are commonly used in personal computers due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. On the other hand, SCSI Bus drives are often found in high-performance servers and workstations due to their faster data transfer rates and ability to support multiple devices on the same bus. Each type of drive caters to different market segments based on their specific requirements and capabilities.</p></p>
