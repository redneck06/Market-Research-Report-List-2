<p><p>Real-time Mocap Systems are used to capture and analyze human motion in real time for various applications such as film, gaming, and sports. Optical systems use cameras and markers to track movement with high precision and accuracy, making them ideal for professional settings. Non-optical systems, on the other hand, rely on sensors attached to the body or clothing to capture motion, offering greater flexibility but sacrificing some level of precision. Both types of systems have their own advantages and are used based on specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
