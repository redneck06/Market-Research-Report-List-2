<p><strong>The DC Power Jacks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole DC Power Jacks</li><li>Surface Mounting DC Power Jacks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC power jacks come in two main types: Through Hole DC Power Jacks and Surface Mounting DC Power Jacks. Through Hole DC Power Jacks are mounted on a circuit board by inserting their leads through pre-drilled holes and soldering them in place. Surface Mounting DC Power Jacks are soldered directly onto the surface of the circuit board. Both types are commonly used in electronic devices to provide a connection for external power sources. The choice between the two types depends on the design and space constraints of the device.</p></p>
