<p><p>Side-Mount Floor Locks are a type of locking mechanism commonly used in industrial settings where heavy machinery or equipment needs to be secured in place. These locks are mounted on the side of the equipment and engage with the floor to prevent movement. On the other hand, Top-Mount Floor Locks are installed on top of the equipment and function in a similar way, but are mounted on the top surface instead. Both types of locks serve the same purpose of ensuring stability and safety in work environments.</p></p>
