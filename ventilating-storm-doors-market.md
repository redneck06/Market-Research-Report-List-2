<p><p>Ventilating storm doors come in two main market types: Upper Vent Storm Doors and Lower Vent Storm Doors. Upper Vent Storm Doors are designed with a vent located at the top of the door, allowing warm air to escape while keeping cold air out. Lower Vent Storm Doors, on the other hand, have a vent at the bottom of the door to allow fresh air to enter the space while maintaining security and protection from the elements. Both types offer effective ventilation solutions for homes while enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
