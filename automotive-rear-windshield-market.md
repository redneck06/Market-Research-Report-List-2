<p><p>Automotive rear windshield market can be categorized into two main types based on the material used: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset materials are rigid and provide high heat resistance, making them suitable for rear windshields that are exposed to extreme temperatures. Thermoplastic materials, on the other hand, offer flexibility and impact resistance, making them ideal for rear windshields in vehicles that may encounter rough terrain. Both types of materials offer different advantages and cater to specific needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
