<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane spray coatings can be classified into three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and other markets. Solvent-based coatings contain organic solvents that evaporate during the drying process, leaving behind a protective polyurethane film. Water-based coatings use water as a carrier instead of solvents, making them more environmentally friendly. The other market includes niche coatings with specialized properties, such as high flexibility or extreme durability. Each type of polyurethane spray coating offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
