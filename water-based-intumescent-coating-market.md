<p><p>Water-based intumescent coating market types include polymer fiber and natural fiber. Polymer fiber coatings are made from synthetic polymers that expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective layer that insulates and slows down the spread of fire. Natural fiber coatings are derived from organic materials such as wood fibers or cellulose, which also expand when heated to create a barrier against flames. Both types of coatings offer fire resistance and are suitable for various applications in the construction, automotive, and marine industries.</p></p>
