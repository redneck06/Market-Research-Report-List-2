<p><ul><li>Anionic Cleaner</li><li>Non-Ionic Cleaner</li><li>Amphoteric Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Skin care cleaner ingredients market is categorized into three main types: anionic cleaner, non-ionic cleaner, and amphoteric cleaner. Anionic cleaners contain negatively charged ions and are effective in removing oil and dirt from the skin. Non-ionic cleaners do not contain charged ions and are gentle on the skin, making them suitable for sensitive skin types. Amphoteric cleaners have both positive and negative charges, providing a balanced and mild cleansing effect. Each type offers distinct benefits for different skin care needs.</p></p>
