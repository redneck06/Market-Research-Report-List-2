<p><p>Permanent Magnet Filters are of two main types: SMS Type and MS Type. SMS Type filters are self-cleaning filters that use a rotating magnetic drum to attract and capture ferrous particles from liquids, making them ideal for industrial applications where continuous operation is required. On the other hand, MS Type filters use a fixed magnetic grid to efficiently remove ferrous contaminants from dry or liquid products, making them suitable for smaller-scale or less demanding operations. Both filter types offer effective and long-lasting magnetic filtration solutions for various industries.</p></p>
