<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Kicking Strap</li><li>Rigid Kicking Strap</li><li>Reverse Thrust Kicking Strap</li><li>Pneumatic Kicking Strap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kicking straps are essential components in sailing to help prevent the boom from rising uncontrollably when sailing downwind. Four main types are available on the market - hydraulic kicking straps use hydraulic pressure to control the tension, rigid kicking straps are fixed and do not allow for adjustment, reverse thrust kicking straps use a lever system to control tension, and pneumatic kicking straps use compressed air to control tension. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different sailing conditions and preferences.</p></p>
