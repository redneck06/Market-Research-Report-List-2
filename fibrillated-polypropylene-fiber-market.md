<p><p>Fibrillated Polypropylene Fiber is available in two main types - Monofilament Fiber and Multifilament Fiber. Multifilament Fiber is made up of multiple filaments twisted together, offering increased flexibility and versatility. Both types are widely used in various industries such as construction, textiles, and agriculture for applications like reinforcement in concrete, soil stabilization, erosion control, and filtration.
