<p><p>The handheld airborne particle counter market can be classified into ionizing and non-ionizing types. Ionizing particle counters use technology that creates charged ions to attract particles for detection. Non-ionizing particle counters do not use ionizing technology and instead rely on light scattering or light obscuration methods to detect particles. Both types of particle counters have their own advantages and applications, depending on the specific needs of the user and the environment in which they will be used.</p></p>
