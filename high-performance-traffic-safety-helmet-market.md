<p><p>High Performance Traffic Safety Helmets are available in two main types: Full Face Helmet and Open Face Helmet. Full Face Helmets provide maximum protection by covering the entire head, including the chin and face. They are often preferred by those who engage in high-speed activities like racing. On the other hand, Open Face Helmets offer more ventilation and visibility while still providing essential protection for the top and back of the head.
