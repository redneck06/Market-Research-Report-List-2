<p><p>Chip resistor R CHIP market types can be divided into pressure-sensitive and thermosensitive categories. Pressure-sensitive chip resistors are designed to respond to changes in pressure, allowing for applications in pressure sensor devices. On the other hand, thermosensitive chip resistors are sensitive to changes in temperature, making them ideal for use in temperature sensor devices. Both types of chip resistors play a crucial role in various electronic applications where precise control and monitoring of pressure or temperature are required.</p></p>
