<p><strong>The Transportation Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Pre-Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transportation coating market consists of four main types: powder, solvent-based, water-based, and pre-treatment coatings. Powder coatings are applied electrostatically and cured under heat. Solvent-based coatings contain volatile organic compounds and require good ventilation during application. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and offer low VOC emissions. Pre-treatment coatings are used to prepare the surface before applying the final coating. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to the diverse needs of the transportation industry.</p></p>
