<p><p>Residential Connected Lighting Market Types include WiFi, ZigBee, Z-Wave, Bluetooth, and Thread. WiFi enables seamless integration with existing home networks, allowing for easy control and automation. ZigBee is known for its low power consumption and ability to create mesh networks for widespread coverage. Z-Wave offers secure and reliable communication between devices within the home, while Bluetooth provides short-range connectivity for simple setups. Thread is a newer protocol that focuses on security and scalability for smart home devices.</p></p>
