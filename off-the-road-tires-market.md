<p><p>Off-the-road tires market consists of three main types: Pneumatic tires, Foam Filling tires, and Others. Pneumatic tires are filled with air, providing a cushioning effect for better shock absorption. Foam Filling tires are filled with foam instead of air, making them puncture-proof and ideal for heavy-duty applications. The Others category includes specialty tires designed for specific terrains or purposes, such as solid tires or hybrid tires. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for different off-road conditions.</p></p>
