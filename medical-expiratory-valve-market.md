<p><p>The medical expiratory valve market can be categorized into single-use and reusable valves. Single-use valves are designed for one-time use and are disposed of after each patient to prevent cross-contamination. Reusable valves, on the other hand, can be sterilized and reused multiple times. Single-use valves are cost-effective and convenient for healthcare facilities with high patient turnover, while reusable valves are more environmentally friendly and can be more economical in the long run. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper ventilation during medical procedures.</p></p>
