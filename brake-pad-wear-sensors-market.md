<p><p>Brake pad wear sensors are a vital component in modern vehicles that alert drivers when their brake pads are worn down. There are two main types of brake pad wear sensors: electronic sensors and audible sensors.</p><p>Electronic pad wear sensors use electronic signals to indicate when brake pads need to be replaced, providing a warning light on the dashboard. Audible pad wear sensors, on the other hand, emit a high-pitched squealing noise when the brake pads are worn down. Both types of sensors help drivers ensure the safety and efficiency of their braking system.</p></p>
