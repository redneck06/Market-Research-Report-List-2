<p><p>Bone conduction headsets come in two main market types: wired and wireless. Wired bone conduction headsets are connected to devices through physical cables, providing reliable connectivity and uninterrupted audio transmission. On the other hand, wireless bone conduction headsets connect to devices using Bluetooth technology, offering more freedom of movement and convenience. Both types of headsets allow users to listen to audio through vibrations sent directly to the skull, bypassing the eardrum for a unique listening experience.</p></p>
