<p><p>The medical exhalation valve market consists of two main types - single-use and reusable valves. Single-use valves are designed for one-time use only and are disposed of after each patient interaction, helping to prevent cross-contamination and reduce the risk of infections. Reusable valves, on the other hand, can be sterilized and used multiple times, making them a cost-effective and environmentally-friendly option for healthcare facilities. Both types of valves play a crucial role in maintaining proper ventilation and respiratory health for patients undergoing medical treatment.</p></p>
