<p><p>Flame Retardant PPA (polyphthalamide) can be divided into two main types based on their composition: Halogen Type and Halogen Free Type. On the other hand, Halogen Free Type flame retardants do not contain these halogen elements, making them a more environmentally friendly option. Both types are used in various industries to improve fire safety in products such as electronics, automotive parts, and construction materials.</p></p>
