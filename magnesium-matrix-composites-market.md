<p><p>Magnesium matrix composites are categorized into two main types: Cast Type and Wrought Type. The Cast Type involves the casting process where molten magnesium is poured into a mold to form the desired shape. This type is commonly used for simple shapes and components. On the other hand, the Wrought Type involves the deformation of solid magnesium through processes like rolling, forging, or extrusion to create complex shapes and structures. This type is preferred for applications requiring higher strength and performance.</p></p>
