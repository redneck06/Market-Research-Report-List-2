<p><p>Styrene Copolymers can be classified into different types based on their composition and properties. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is a common thermoplastic used in various applications. ASA (Acrylonitrile Styrene Acrylate) offers high weather resistance, making it suitable for outdoor use. SAN (Styrene Acrylonitrile) combines the properties of styrene and acrylonitrile for enhanced heat resistance. MABS (Methacrylate Butadiene Styrene) offers improved impact resistance compared to ABS. Other types of Styrene Copolymers cater to specific niche markets with unique requirements.</p></p>
