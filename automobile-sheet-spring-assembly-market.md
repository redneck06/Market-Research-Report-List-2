<p><strong>The Automobile Sheet Spring Assembly Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-leaf Springs</li><li>Mono-leaf Springs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile sheet spring assemblies are categorized into two main types: Multi-leaf springs and Mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple layers of leaf springs stacked on top of each other, providing better load-carrying capacity and strength. On the other hand, Mono-leaf springs are made up of a single piece of tapered spring steel, offering a smoother ride and better handling. Both types cater to different market demands and preferences, with Multi-leaf springs being more commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles and Mono-leaf springs in lighter vehicles.</p></p>
