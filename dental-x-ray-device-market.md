<p><p>The Dental X ray Device market consists of two main types: Intraoral X-Ray Imaging and Extraoral X-Ray Imaging. Intraoral X-Ray Imaging devices are placed inside the patient's mouth to capture detailed images of individual teeth and their surrounding structures. Extraoral X-Ray Imaging devices are placed outside of the patient's mouth to capture broader images of the entire mouth, jaw, and skull. Both types of devices are crucial tools for dental professionals in diagnosing and treating various oral health conditions.</p></p>
