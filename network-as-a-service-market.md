<p><p>Network-as-a-Service market consists of various types including LANaaS (LAN-as-a-Service) and WANaaS (WAN-as-a-Service). LANaaS provides companies with a virtual local area network that is hosted and maintained by a third party, offering flexibility and scalability without the need for physical hardware. On the other hand, WANaaS provides wide area network services over a virtual connection, allowing businesses to access data and applications from multiple locations securely and efficiently. Both LANaaS and WANaaS offer cost-effective solutions for networking needs.</p></p>
