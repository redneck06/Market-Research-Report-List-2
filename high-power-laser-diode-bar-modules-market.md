<p><p>High-power laser diode bar modules are typically divided into two main types based on their operation: pulsed laser diode bar and continuous wave (CW) laser diode bar. </p><p>Pulsed laser diode bars are designed to emit high-power laser pulses for applications such as material processing, telecommunications, and medical devices. On the other hand, CW laser diode bars produce a continuous output of laser light for tasks like laser cutting, welding, and pumping solid-state lasers. Both types offer high optical power and efficiency, making them critical components in a variety of industries.</p></p>
