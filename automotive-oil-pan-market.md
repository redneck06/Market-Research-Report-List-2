<p><strong>The Automotive Oil Pan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structural Oil Pan</li><li>Non-Structural Oil Pan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive oil pan market is divided into two main types: structural oil pan and non-structural oil pan. Structural oil pans are an integral component of the engine block, providing structural support and rigidity to the engine assembly. On the other hand, non-structural oil pans are standalone components mounted underneath the engine to store and distribute oil throughout the engine. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining proper lubrication and temperature control in automotive engines, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
