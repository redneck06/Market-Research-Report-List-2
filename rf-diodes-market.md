<p><p>RF diodes are semiconductor devices used in radio frequency circuits for various applications. The market types include amplification diodes, which amplify RF signals; detector diodes, which convert RF signals to DC for detection; mixer diodes, which perform frequency conversion in RF circuits; damper diodes, which suppress RF interference; and limiter diodes, which limit the power of RF signals. Each type of diode plays a crucial role in RF circuits, contributing to the overall performance and functionality of the system.</p></p>
