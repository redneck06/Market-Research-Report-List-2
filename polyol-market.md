<p><strong>The Polyol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyester Polyol</li><li>Polyether Polyol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyol market is divided into two main types: Polyester Polyol and Polyether Polyol. Polyester Polyol is derived from the reaction of an organic diacid with an organic glycol, resulting in a viscous liquid with good mechanical properties and resistance to heat. On the other hand, Polyether Polyol is produced through the reaction of an epoxide with an initiator compound, yielding a flexible and elastic material suitable for use in products like adhesives and sealants. Both types of polyols play a crucial role in various industries such as construction, automotive, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>
