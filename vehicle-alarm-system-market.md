<p><p>The vehicle alarm system market includes various types of security features such as central locking, biometric capture device, remote keyless entry, steering lock, alarm, and immobilizer. Central locking allows all doors to be locked or unlocked simultaneously, while biometric capture devices use fingerprint recognition for access control. Remote keyless entry enables keyless entry and ignition, while steering locks prevent steering wheel movement. Alarms alert the owner of unauthorized access, and immobilizers prevent the engine from starting without proper authorization.</p></p>
