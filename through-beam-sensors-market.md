<p><p>Through Beam Sensors are a type of sensor used for detecting the presence of an object by transmitting a beam of light from a transmitter to a receiver. Through Beam Sensors are widely used in industrial automation, automotive, packaging, and various other industries for object detection, counting, and positioning.</p><p>The Through Beam Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.10% during the forecast period. <p><p>Through beam sensors are available in two main types, Infrared and Laser type sensors. Infrared type sensors use infrared light to detect objects passing through, while Laser type sensors use laser beams for the same purpose. Both types offer accurate and reliable object detection in various industrial applications. Infrared sensors are known for their cost-effectiveness and simplicity, while Laser sensors are preferred for their high precision and long sensing range. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for through beam sensors.</p></p>
