<p><p>Quantum Dot Display Plates are a type of display technology that use semiconductor nanocrystals to produce vibrant and high-quality images. Within this market, there are two main types: QLED (Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diode) and QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film). QLED displays directly integrate quantum dots into the display panel, offering improved color accuracy and brightness. QDEF displays, on the other hand, use a film containing quantum dots to enhance the color performance of traditional LCD displays. Both technologies are gaining popularity in the market for their ability to deliver superior picture quality.</p></p>
