<p><ul><li>Single Blade Board Edgers</li><li>Twin Blade Board Edgers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Board edgers are machines used in lumber mills to straighten and trim the edges of boards. Single blade board edgers are simpler machines with one blade that are typically used for smaller operations. Twin blade board edgers are more advanced machines with two blades that can process larger volumes of lumber at a faster pace. Single blade board edgers are more affordable and easier to maintain, while twin blade board edgers are more efficient and suited for high production mills.</p></p>
