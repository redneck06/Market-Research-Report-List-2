<p><p>The automotive EGR system market consists of various components including EGR valves, EGR coolers, EGR sensors, and ECU (Engine Control Unit). EGR valves help control exhaust gas recirculation, while EGR coolers reduce the temperature of recirculated gas. EGR sensors monitor gas flow and composition, while the ECU controls the overall functioning of the system. Each component plays a crucial role in reducing emissions and improving fuel efficiency in vehicles, contributing to the overall performance of the automotive EGR system.</p></p>
