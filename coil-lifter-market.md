<p><p>Coil lifters are specialized lifting equipment used in industries for handling coils of metal or other materials. The three main types are Double Leg Coil Lifters, C-Hook Coil Lifters, and U-Hook Coil Lifters. Double Leg Coil Lifters provide balanced lifting for heavier coils, while C-Hook Coil Lifters are designed for handling coils with irregular shapes. U-Hook Coil Lifters are used for lifting coils with eyelets or holes. These different types cater to specific needs in various industries where coil lifting is required.</p></p>
