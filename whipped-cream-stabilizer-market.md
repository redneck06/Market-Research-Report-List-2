<p><p>Whipped cream stabilizers are categorized into different types based on their ingredients and functionalities. Starch type stabilizers use modified starches to enhance stability and texture of the whipped cream. Xanthan gum type stabilizers are derived from sugar and provide excellent thickening properties. Guar gum type stabilizers are derived from guar beans and help improve the consistency of whipped cream. Other types of stabilizers may include gelatin, carrageenan, or agar, each offering unique benefits for stabilizing whipped cream.</p></p>
