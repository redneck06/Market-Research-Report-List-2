<p><ul><li>TDI Polyurethane Prepolymer</li><li>MDI Polyurethane Prepolymer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane Prepolymers (PPU) are classified into different types based on the type of isocyanate used in their production. TDI Polyurethane Prepolymers are made with toluene diisocyanate, while MDI Polyurethane Prepolymers use methylene diphenyl diisocyanate. The "Others" category includes Prepolymers made with different types of isocyanates. Each type of PPU offers unique properties and characteristics, catering to various end-user applications in industries such as automotive, construction, furniture, and electronics.</p></p>
