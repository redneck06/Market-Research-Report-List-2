<p><p>Wireless inkjet printers come in two main types - solid inkjet and liquid inkjet. Solid inkjet printers use solid ink sticks that are melted and then transferred onto the paper, resulting in vibrant and consistent colors. Liquid inkjet printers, on the other hand, use liquid ink that is sprayed onto the paper in small droplets. Both types of printers offer wireless connectivity, allowing users to easily print documents from various devices without the need for physical cables.</p></p>
