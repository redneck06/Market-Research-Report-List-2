<p><p>AC DC Cable Assembly Market consists of four types of cables - Flexible, Semi-Flexible, Semi-Rigid, and Rigid Cables. Flexible Cables are designed for applications requiring frequent bending or movement. Semi-Flexible Cables offer some flexibility while providing more stability and strength. Semi-Rigid Cables are less flexible than the previous two types, providing more stability and less bending ability. Rigid Cables are the least flexible, offering maximum stability and strength but minimal bending capability. Each type caters to different needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
