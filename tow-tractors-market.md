<p><p>Tow tractors have three main market types based on their design and usage. Pedestrian towing tractors are operated by a walking operator and are ideal for light-duty tasks in confined spaces. Stand-in towing tractors allow the operator to stand on a platform while driving, providing better visibility and ergonomics. Rider-seated towing tractors are designed for longer distances and heavier loads, with the operator sitting on a seat for increased comfort and control. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
