<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Wound Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Multilayer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Frequency Inductors market consists of three main types: Wire Wound Type, Film Type, and Multilayer Type. Wire Wound Type inductors are made by winding wire around a core, providing high inductance values. Film Type inductors are made by depositing a thin film of conductive material on a substrate, offering precise inductance control. Multilayer Type inductors consist of multiple layers of conductive material sandwiched between insulating layers, allowing for compact design and high frequency performance. Each type serves different applications within the RF industry.</p></p>
