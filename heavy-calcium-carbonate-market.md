<p><p>The Heavy Calcium Carbonate market can be divided into two types based on the production process: Dry Process and Wet Process. In the Dry Process, calcium carbonate is produced by directly crushing natural limestone, while in the Wet Process, calcium carbonate is produced by using water to react with limestone. The Dry Process market is preferred for its lower production costs and energy consumption, while the Wet Process market is favored for its higher purity and better particle size distribution.</p></p>
