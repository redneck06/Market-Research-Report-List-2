<p><p>The Automotive Side Airbag Device market includes two main types - Side Torso Airbags and Side Tubular/Curtain Airbags. Side Torso Airbags are designed to protect the driver and passengers' upper body in the event of a side impact collision. On the other hand, Side Tubular or Curtain Airbags are designed to protect the head and neck area by deploying from the ceiling or door during a crash. Both types of airbags work together to provide comprehensive protection in different areas of the vehicle during a collision.</p></p>
