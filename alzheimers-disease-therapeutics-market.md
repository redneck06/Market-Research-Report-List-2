<p><p>Alzheimer's Disease Therapeutics market includes three main types of treatments: biomarkers, cholinesterase inhibitors, and NMDA receptor antagonists. NMDA receptor antagonists target glutamate receptors to help regulate brain function. These different types of treatments aim to slow down the progression of Alzheimer's Disease and improve quality of life for patients.</p></p>
