<p><p>HEFA-SPK, FT-SPK, ATJ-SPK, Co-processing, and Others are types of biomass-based renewable jet fuel markets. HEFA-SPK refers to Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids-Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene. FT-SPK stands for Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene. ATJ-SPK is Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene. Co-processing involves blending biomass-derived components with traditional jet fuel. Other types may include pyrolysis-based or hydrothermal liquefaction-based jet fuels. These markets offer sustainable alternatives to conventional jet fuels, helping reduce greenhouse gas emissions in the aviation industry.</p></p>
