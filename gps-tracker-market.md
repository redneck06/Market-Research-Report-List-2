<p><strong>The GPS Tracker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone Tracker</li><li>OBD Trackers</li><li>Advanced Trackers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPS Tracker market is categorized into three types: Standalone Trackers, OBD Trackers, and Advanced Trackers. Standalone Trackers can be used independently without the need for a vehicle's OBD port, making them versatile for various tracking purposes. OBD Trackers are plugged into a vehicle's OBD port to provide real-time monitoring of vehicle diagnostics and location. Advanced Trackers offer more advanced features such as geofencing, remote engine shutdown, and fuel monitoring, making them suitable for fleet management and high-security applications.</p></p>
