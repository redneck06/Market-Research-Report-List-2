<p><strong>The Screw Doser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Screw Doser</li><li>Gravimetric Screw Doser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Screw Doser market is divided into two main types: Volumetric Screw Doser and Gravimetric Screw Doser. Volumetric Screw Doser measures the quantity of material based on volume, making it suitable for applications where precision is not critical. On the other hand, Gravimetric Screw Doser measures the quantity based on weight, providing more accurate and consistent results. Both types cater to different industries and applications, with Gravimetric Screw Doser being preferred for applications that require strict dosing accuracy.</p></p>
