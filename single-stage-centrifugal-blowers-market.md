<p><ul><li>Low Speed</li><li>High Speed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-stage centrifugal blowers are commonly categorized into two types based on their speed: low speed and high speed blowers. Low speed blowers are typically designed for applications that require higher air flow rates at lower pressure differentials, while high speed blowers are suited for applications where higher pressure differentials are needed at lower air flow rates. Low speed blowers are more energy efficient, quieter, and have longer lifespans, making them ideal for residential and commercial HVAC systems. High speed blowers, on the other hand, are often used in industrial settings where higher pressures are required for processes such as pneumatic conveying or material handling.</p></p>
