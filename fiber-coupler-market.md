<p><strong>The Fiber Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multimode Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber couplers are devices used in optical communication networks to split or combine optical signals. Single mode couplers are designed for use with single mode fibers, which have a smaller core size and are ideal for long-distance transmission. Multimode couplers, on the other hand, are designed for use with multimode fibers, which have a larger core size and are typically used for shorter distances. Both types of couplers play a crucial role in ensuring efficient signal transmission in fiber optic networks.</p></p>
