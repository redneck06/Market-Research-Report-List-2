<p><p>HDPE Microduct Market Types include Direct Install Type, Direct Burial Type, and Flame Retardant Type. Direct Install Type microducts are designed for easy and quick installation using push-fit connectors. Direct Burial Type microducts are specifically designed for underground installation, providing protection against environmental factors. Flame Retardant Type microducts are engineered to meet strict safety standards, preventing the spread of fire in case of emergencies. Each type caters to different installation requirements and offers unique benefits for various applications.</p></p>
