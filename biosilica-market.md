<p><p>Highly dispersible silica and easy dispersible silica are two main types of biosilica market. Highly dispersible silica has smaller particle size which allows for better dispersion in various applications such as rubber, oral care, and coatings. On the other hand, easy dispersible silica has larger particle size and offers improved flowability and handling in applications like pharmaceuticals and food products. Both types of biosilica play a key role in enhancing the performance and properties of products across different industries.</p></p>
