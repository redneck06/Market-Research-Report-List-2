<p><p>A handheld thermal coagulator is a medical device used for coagulation of tissue during surgery or medical procedures. There are two main types of handheld thermal coagulators: unipolar and multipolar. Unipolar coagulators use a single electrode to deliver electrical current to the tissue, while multipolar coagulators use multiple electrodes to provide a more precise and controlled thermal coagulation. Both types are widely used in various surgical specialties for hemostasis and tissue sealing.</p></p>
