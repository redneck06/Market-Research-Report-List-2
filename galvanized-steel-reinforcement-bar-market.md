<p><p>Galvanized steel reinforcement bar market includes two main types: Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar and Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar. The Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar is dipped in a bath of molten zinc to create a protective coating. Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar undergoes a more efficient and automated process, resulting in a more uniform coating. Both types offer corrosion protection for steel reinforcement bars used in construction, infrastructure, and other applications.</p></p>
