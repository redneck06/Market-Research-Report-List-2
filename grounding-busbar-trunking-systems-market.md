<p><p>Grounding busbar trunking systems are used for efficient power distribution in buildings and industrial facilities. Nonsegregated bus duct systems have all phases in a single compartment, making them more compact and cost-effective. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the application, with segregated bus ducts being preferred for critical or high-power installations, while nonsegregated bus ducts are suitable for less demanding environments.</p></p>
