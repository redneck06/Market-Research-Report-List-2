<p><p>Trace Metal Analysis Instruments are used for detecting and measuring trace amounts of metals in various samples. Four main types of instruments dominate the market: Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy, X-Ray Fluorescence, ICP-MS (Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry), and others. Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy is known for its high sensitivity and selectivity, while X-Ray Fluorescence offers rapid analysis of multiple elements. ICP-MS is favored for its high precision and low detection limits. Other types of instruments may include ICP-OES (Inductively Coupled Plasma Optical Emission Spectroscopy) and voltammetry.</p></p>
