<p><p>Vehicle Ultrasonic radar is typically categorized into two main types: Forward Radar and Reversing Radar. Forward radar systems are installed on the front of the vehicle and are designed to detect obstacles in the vehicle's forward path. These systems help in providing collision warnings and assist with features like adaptive cruise control. On the other hand, reversing radar systems are installed on the rear of the vehicle and help drivers in detecting obstacles while reversing, thereby preventing accidents and ensuring safety during parking maneuvers.</p></p>
