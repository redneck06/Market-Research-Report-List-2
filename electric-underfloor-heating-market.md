<p><ul><li>Heating Cable</li><li>Electrothermal</li><li>Carbon Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric underfloor heating market includes three main types: Heating Cable, Electrothermal, and Carbon Crystal. </p><p>- Heating Cable systems consist of cables that are installed beneath the flooring to generate heat.</p><p>- Electrothermal systems use heating mats that are installed directly under the flooring to provide heat.</p><p>- Carbon Crystal systems use carbon crystal panels that are installed under the flooring to produce heat. </p><p>Each type offers different benefits in terms of efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and ease of installation.</p></p>
