<p><strong>The Perilla Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toasted Perilla Oil</li><li>Untoasted Perilla Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perilla oil comes in two main types: toasted and untoasted. Toasted perilla oil is made from roasted perilla seeds, giving it a nutty and robust flavor perfect for drizzling over dishes or adding to marinades. Untoasted perilla oil, on the other hand, is made from raw perilla seeds and has a milder taste, making it versatile for both cooking and dressing. Both types of perilla oil are known for their high levels of omega-3 fatty acids and anti-inflammatory properties, making them popular choices in healthy cooking.</p></p>
