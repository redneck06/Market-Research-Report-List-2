<p><strong>The Automotive Wiper Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bone Wiper</li><li>Boneless Wiper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive wiper systems market consists of two main types: bone wiper and boneless wiper. Bone wipers have metal frame arms that hold the rubber blade in a traditional way, providing good performance at a lower cost. On the other hand, boneless wipers use a spring steel band to apply uniform pressure along the length of the blade, allowing for better windshield contact and improved cleaning efficiency. Both types have their own advantages and are commonly used in different vehicles based on the desired level of performance and cost.</p></p>
