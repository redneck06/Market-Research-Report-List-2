<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Refrigerant Gas Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) Sensors</li><li>Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refrigerant gas sensor modules are used to detect refrigerant gas leaks in various applications. NDIR sensors utilize infrared light to analyze the concentration of gas by measuring the amount of infrared light absorbed. These sensors are highly accurate and reliable. On the other hand, Metal Oxide Semiconductor sensors detect gas by measuring changes in conductivity when the gas interacts with a heated metal oxide surface. While they are cost-effective, they are less accurate compared to NDIR sensors.</p></p>
