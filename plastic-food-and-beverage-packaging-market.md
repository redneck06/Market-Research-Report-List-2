<p><p>Plastic food and beverage packaging can be categorized into two main types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging includes materials like films, wraps, and pouches that can bend and conform to the shape of the product. It is often used for items like snacks and ready-to-eat meals. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, includes materials like bottles, jars, and containers that do not easily change their shape. This type of packaging is commonly used for beverages and larger food items.</p></p>
