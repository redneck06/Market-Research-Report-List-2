<p><p>Swellable packers are used in oil and gas well completion operations for zonal isolation and wellbore stability. Permanent packers are designed to remain in place permanently once installed, providing a permanent seal between different zones in the wellbore. Retrievable packers, on the other hand, can be easily removed from the wellbore when needed. Both types of packers are essential tools in well completion operations, allowing for efficient and effective zonal isolation in oil and gas wells.</p></p>
