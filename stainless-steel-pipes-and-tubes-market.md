<p><p>Stainless steel pipes and tubes market can be classified into two main types: seamless pipes and tubes, and welded pipes and tubes. Seamless pipes and tubes are made by extruding a solid cylinder of stainless steel through a die to create the final shape without any joints. On the other hand, welded pipes and tubes are created by welding together stainless steel strips or plates to form the desired shape. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
