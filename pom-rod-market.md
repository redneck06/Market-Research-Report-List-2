<p><p>POM rods, or polyoxymethylene rods, are available in two main market types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene refers to POM rods made from a single type of polymer, providing high strength and stiffness. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene involves blending different polymers to enhance properties such as impact resistance and chemical stability. Both market types cater to various industrial applications requiring durable and versatile materials, offering different characteristics to meet specific needs.</p></p>
