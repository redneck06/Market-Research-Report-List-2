<p><strong>The Light Truck Tire(LT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light Truck Tires (LT) are divided into two main market types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias tires are constructed with crisscross layers of cords, providing higher durability and better load-carrying capacity. On the other hand, Radial Tires have cords that run at 90-degree angles from the direction of travel, offering improved traction and fuel efficiency. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of drivers, with Bias Tires being more cost-effective and Radial Tires being more performance-oriented.</p></p>
