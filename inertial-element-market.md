<p><ul><li>Capacitance</li><li>Inductance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inertial element market includes capacitance and inductance types. Capacitance elements store electrical energy by holding a charge between two conductive plates separated by a dielectric material. They are commonly used in filters, sensors, and oscillators. Inductance elements store energy in a magnetic field created by a coil of wire. They are often used in transformers, motors, and generators. Both types play important roles in electronic circuits by controlling the flow of current and voltage.</p></p>
