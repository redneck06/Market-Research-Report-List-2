<p><strong>The Automotive Catalytic Converter Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalysts (TWC)</li><li>Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC)</li><li>Lean NOx Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive catalytic converter equipment market consists of various types including Three-way Catalysts (TWC) which reduce emissions of harmful gases like carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides, and hydrocarbons. Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC) reduce nitrogen oxide emissions in lean-burn engines. Lean NOx Trap (LNT) captures and holds nitrogen oxides until they can be converted into nitrogen. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) converts harmful gases into less harmful substances. Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) convert carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons into less harmful gases in diesel engines.</p></p>
