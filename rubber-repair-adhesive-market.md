<p><p>Rubber repair adhesive market is divided into two types: Hot Bond and Cold Bond. Hot Bond adhesive requires heat to activate and bond the rubber materials, typically used for large industrial repair applications. In contrast, Cold Bond adhesive does not require heat and is more suitable for smaller repairs and applications where heat is not available or practical. Both types of adhesive offer strong bonding capabilities and durability for repairing rubber materials effectively.</p></p>
