<p><p>Low iron clear glass is a type of high-quality, ultra-clear glass that is used in various applications due to its exceptional clarity and transparency. It is available in two main market types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is made by pouring molten glass onto a metal table and rolling it into a sheet, resulting in a textured finish. Float glass, on the other hand, is made by floating molten glass on a layer of molten tin, producing a smooth and uniform surface. Both types of low iron clear glass are popular in architectural and design industries for their aesthetic appeal and performance.</p></p>
