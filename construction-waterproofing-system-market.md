<p><p>Construction Waterproofing System Market Types include Liquid Waterproofing Membrane, Cementitious Waterproofing, Bituminous Waterproofing, and Polyurethane Waterproofing. Liquid Waterproofing Membrane is a thin, flexible layer applied to surfaces to prevent water penetration. Cementitious Waterproofing involves using a mixture of cement and additives to create a waterproof barrier. Bituminous Waterproofing uses bitumen-based materials for water resistance. Polyurethane Waterproofing involves using a durable, synthetic material that forms a seamless waterproof membrane. These different types cater to various construction needs and provide effective waterproofing solutions.</p></p>
