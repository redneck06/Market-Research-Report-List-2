<p><p>Capillary electrophoresis instruments are classified into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic capillary electrophoresis instruments offer high-throughput analysis capabilities, automated sample injection, and data collection, making them ideal for laboratories with high sample volumes. Conversely, semi-automatic capillary electrophoresis instruments require more manual intervention in terms of sample loading and data analysis. This type is often preferred in smaller laboratories or research settings where lower sample volumes are processed.</p></p>
