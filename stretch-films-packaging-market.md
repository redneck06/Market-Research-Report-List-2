<p><p>Stretch Films Packaging Market Types refer to the different materials used for packaging products. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) is a commonly used material known for its flexibility and strength. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is another popular choice due to its durability and ability to resist punctures. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) is a cost-effective option with high clarity and good barrier properties. Polypropylene (PP) is a versatile material known for its high tensile strength. Other materials used include polyethylene terephthalate (PET), nylon, and ethylene vinyl alcohol (EVOH).</p></p>
