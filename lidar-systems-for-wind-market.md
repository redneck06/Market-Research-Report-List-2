<p><strong>The Lidar Systems for Wind Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground-mounted Lidar systems</li><li>Nacelle-mounted Lidar systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lidar systems for wind market can be classified into two main types: ground-mounted Lidar systems and nacelle-mounted Lidar systems. Ground-mounted Lidar systems are installed on the ground and measure the wind characteristics at various heights above the ground. On the other hand, nacelle-mounted Lidar systems are installed on the nacelle of the wind turbine itself, providing a more precise measurement of the wind conditions at the specific location where the turbine is located. Both types of Lidar systems play a crucial role in optimizing wind farm performance and maximizing energy output.</p></p>
