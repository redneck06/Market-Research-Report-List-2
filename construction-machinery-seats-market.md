<p><p>Construction machinery seats come in two main types: mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats use springs and shock absorbers to provide a comfortable ride for operators. Air suspension seats, on the other hand, use compressed air to adjust seat height and cushioning based on the operator's weight and preference. Both types of seats are designed to reduce operator fatigue and improve overall comfort during long hours of operation on construction machinery.</p></p>
