<p><p>Kinesiology muscle tape is available in two main market types: roll shape and precut shape. Roll shape tape comes in a continuous roll that can be cut to the desired length for a customized fit. Precut shape tape, on the other hand, is pre-measured and pre-cut into specific shapes and sizes for easy application. Both types of tape offer support and stability to muscles and joints during physical activity, but the precut shape tape is more convenient and time-saving for users.</p></p>
