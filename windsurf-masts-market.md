<p><p>The Windsurf Masts market is divided into two main types: SDM (Standard Diameter Mast) and RDM (Reduced Diameter Mast). SDM masts have a larger diameter and are stiffer, providing more power for larger sails and heavier riders. RDM masts have a smaller diameter and are more flexible, making them ideal for smaller sails and lighter riders. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on personal preferences, riding style, and sail compatibility.</p></p>
