<p><ul><li>Conventional Annunciators</li><li>Dedicated Annunciators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Annunciators market includes two main types: Conventional Annunciators and Dedicated Annunciators. Conventional Annunciators are basic alarm systems that display the status of various processes through individual flashing lights or text messages. They are simple and cost-effective solutions used in various industries. Dedicated Annunciators, on the other hand, are customized systems designed for specific applications or industries. They offer advanced features such as touchscreen displays, customizable alarm settings, and integration with other control systems. Dedicated Annunciators tend to be more complex and expensive but provide enhanced functionality and control.</p></p>
