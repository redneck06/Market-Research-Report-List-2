<p><p>Warehouse Control Systems (WCS) are available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise systems are installed and maintained on the company's own servers, providing full control and customization but requiring heavy investment in hardware and IT infrastructure. Cloud-Based systems, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. Businesses can choose the type of WCS that best suits their needs and budget.</p></p>
