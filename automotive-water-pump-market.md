<p><p>The automotive water pump market can be classified into two main types: Mechanical Water Pump and Electric Water Pump. Mechanical water pumps are driven directly by the engine using a belt and pulley system, while electric water pumps are operated using an electric motor. Mechanical water pumps are more traditionally used in older vehicles, whereas electric water pumps are becoming increasingly popular due to their efficiency and ability to be controlled electronically. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the proper circulation of coolant in a vehicle's cooling system.</p></p>
