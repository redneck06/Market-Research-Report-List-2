<p><strong>The 3D Vision Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereo Vision</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stereo vision and structured light are two types of 3D vision sensor technologies used in various industries. Stereo vision systems use two cameras to create a 3D image by comparing the disparities between the two images. This technology is widely used in robotics, automotive, and healthcare industries for tasks such as object detection and navigation. On the other hand, structured light systems project a pattern of light onto an object and analyze the distortion of the pattern to create a 3D image. This technology is popular in quality control, manufacturing, and AR/VR applications.</p></p>
