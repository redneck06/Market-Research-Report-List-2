CFRP is known for its lightweight and high strength properties, making it ideal for structural components. CMCs are designed for high-temperature applications such as engine parts, while MMCs combine carbon fiber with metal for enhanced durability. These materials offer significant advantages in terms of performance, efficiency, and sustainability in the automotive industry.</p></p>
