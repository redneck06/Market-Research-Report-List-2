<p><p>IoT motion light sensors are available in two main market types - wired sensors and wireless sensors. Wireless sensors, on the other hand, communicate through radio frequencies or WiFi, providing flexibility and easy installation without the need for complex wiring. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, with wired sensors being suitable for permanent installations and wireless sensors offering convenience and scalability.</p></p>
