<p><ul><li>Solvent Based Laminate Adhesive</li><li>Solventless Laminate Adhesive</li><li>Water Based Laminate Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminate adhesive is categorized into three main types: solvent-based, solventless, and water-based. Solvent-based laminate adhesive contains volatile organic compounds and requires special ventilation during application. Solventless laminate adhesive does not contain any solvents and offers a more environmentally friendly option. Water-based laminate adhesive is a non-toxic and easy to use option that is gaining popularity due to its eco-friendly nature. Each type of adhesive offers different benefits and is suited for various applications in the laminate industry.</p></p>
