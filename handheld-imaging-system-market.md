<p><strong>The Handheld Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless Handheld Imaging System</li><li>Wired Handheld Imaging System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld imaging systems are divided into two main types: wireless handheld imaging systems and wired handheld imaging systems. </p><p>Wireless handheld imaging systems do not require any physical connection to a device or computer, allowing for greater flexibility and mobility. They use wireless technology to transmit images and data, making them ideal for point-of-care applications and field use.</p><p>On the other hand, wired handheld imaging systems rely on a physical connection to a device or computer through cables. While they may have limitations in terms of mobility, they often offer more stable and secure data transmission for critical imaging tasks.</p></p>
