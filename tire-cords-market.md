<p><p>Tire cords are used in the reinforcement layers of tires to provide strength and stability. The two main types of tire cords are polyester tire cord and hybrid tire cord. Polyester tire cords are made from high-strength polyester fibers, providing good abrasion resistance and dimensional stability. Hybrid tire cords combine different types of fibers such as aramid, nylon, and polyester to achieve specific performance characteristics, such as improved strength and flexibility. Both types of tire cords play a crucial role in ensuring the durability and performance of tires.</p></p>
