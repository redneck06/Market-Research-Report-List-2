<p><p>RF Coaxial Cable Market Types - 50Ω, 75Ω, 93Ω, 95Ω refer to different impedance levels used in RF coaxial cables. The impedance level indicates the resistance to the flow of electrical current within the cable. The most common types are 50Ω and 75Ω, with 50Ω primarily used in industrial and military applications, while 75Ω is commonly used in consumer electronics. 93Ω and 95Ω cables are less common but are used in specialized applications where specific impedance requirements need to be met.</p></p>
