Anionic surfactants are known for their ability to create foam and remove dirt and oil from surfaces, making them ideal for cleaning products. On the other hand, cationic surfactants are commonly used in fabric softeners and hair conditioners due to their ability to impart a positive charge and increase absorption of other ingredients. Both types play a crucial role in the formulation of personal care and cleaning products.</p></p>
