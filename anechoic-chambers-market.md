<p><p>Anechoic chambers are used for conducting acoustic testing and experiments. Free sound field space anechoic chambers provide a completely isolated environment with no sound reflections, ideal for precise measurements. Semi-free sound field space anechoic chambers have some sound absorbing material on the walls, allowing for accurate testing in a controlled environment. Both types of chambers are essential for various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics to ensure accurate and reliable acoustic testing results.</p></p>
