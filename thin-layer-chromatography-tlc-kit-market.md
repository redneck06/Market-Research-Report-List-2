<p><p>Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) Kit is a tool used in chemical analysis to separate and identify compounds in a mixture. The kit consists of a thin layer of adsorbent material on a glass or plastic plate, where a small amount of the mixture is applied and then developed with a solvent. The different compounds in the mixture move up the plate at different rates, allowing for their separation and identification.</p><p>The Thin Layer Chromatography TLC Kit Market is experiencing substantial growth, with an expected CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
