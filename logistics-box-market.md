<p><p>Anti-static Logistics Box, Conductive Logistics Box, and Insulated Logistics Box are different types of specialized containers designed to meet specific storage and transportation needs. Anti-static Logistics Boxes are used to prevent static electricity build-up, Conductive Logistics Boxes are ideal for environments where electrostatic discharge is a concern, and Insulated Logistics Boxes are utilized for temperature-sensitive goods. These market types cater to industries such as electronics, pharmaceuticals, and food storage, where proper handling and protection are crucial for the quality and safety of the products being transported.</p></p>
