<p><p>An Artificial Urinary Sphincter (AUS) is a medical device designed to treat urinary incontinence by restoring voluntary control over urination. It consists of three components - a cuff placed around the urethra, a pressure-regulating balloon located in the abdomen, and a pump placed in the scrotum or labia. When the patient wants to urinate, they can manually pump the device to release the cuff and allow urine to be expelled.</p><p>The Artificial Urinary Sphincter Market is witnessing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.60% during the forecast period.
