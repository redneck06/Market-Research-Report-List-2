<p><p>Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD) wire comes in two main types: thin-film RTD and wire-wound RTD. Thin-film RTD wire is made by depositing a thin layer of platinum on a ceramic substrate, providing fast response times and high accuracy. Wire-wound RTD wire, on the other hand, consists of a coil of platinum wire wrapped around a ceramic core, offering higher stability and durability. Both types are widely used in temperature measurement applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
