<p><p>The light vehicle wheel market consists of three main types: casting, forging, and other. Casting wheels are made by pouring molten metal into a mold to create the desired shape. Forged wheels are created by applying high pressure to shape heated metal into the desired form. The "other" category includes alternative manufacturing methods such as flow forming or rotary forging. Each type of wheel manufacturing process offers its own set of advantages and characteristics, catering to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
