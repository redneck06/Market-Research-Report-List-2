<p><p>Carbon fiber is being increasingly used in the wind power industry due to its lightweight and high strength properties. Three main types of carbon fiber used in wind power applications are PAN-based, viscose-based, and pitch-based carbon fiber. PAN-based carbon fiber is the most common type and is known for its high tensile strength. Viscose-based carbon fiber is used for its good electrical conductivity. Pitch-based carbon fiber is preferred for its high modulus. Other types of carbon fiber may include hybrid and specialty fibers tailored for specific wind power applications.</p></p>
