<p><p>Transaxles are utilized in vehicles to transmit power from the engine to the wheels. Non-Disconnected Transaxles combine the transmission and differential into a single unit, providing a compact design and efficient power transfer. Disconnect Transaxles, on the other hand, have the capability to disconnect the transmission from the differential, offering improved fuel efficiency and reduced wear on the components.
