<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fibre Optics Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intensity Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors</li><li>Phase Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors</li><li>Wavelength Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors</li><li>Polarization Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optics sensors are classified based on the modulation technique used to measure physical quantities. Intensity modulated sensors vary the light intensity passing through the fiber. Phase modulated sensors measure changes in the light wave's phase. Wavelength modulated sensors use the light wavelength to detect variations. Polarization modulated sensors analyze changes in the light polarization state. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications, providing a wide range of options for measuring various parameters with high accuracy and sensitivity.</p></p>
