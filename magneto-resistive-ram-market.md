There are two main types of MRAM in the market - Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes a magnetic field to store data, while STT-MRAM uses spin-transfer torque to achieve the same. Toggle MRAM is known for its high speed and endurance, while STT-MRAM offers better scalability and lower power consumption. Both types are seeing increasing adoption in various applications due to their superior performance characteristics.</p></p>
