<p><p>Leakage protectors are available in three main market types: Voltage Type, Current Type, and Pulse Type. Voltage Type protectors are triggered by exceeding a certain voltage threshold, while Current Type protectors are activated when the current exceeds a set limit. Pulse Type protectors are designed to handle sudden spikes or surges in voltage. Each type offers different levels of protection based on the specific needs of the electrical system it is designed to safeguard.</p></p>
