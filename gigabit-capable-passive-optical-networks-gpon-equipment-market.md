<p><p>Gigabit capable Passive Optical Networks (GPON) Equipment is widely used in three main market types: FTTH/O, FTTB+LAN, and FTTB+DSL. FTTH/O refers to Fiber-to-the-Home/Office, where the optical network extends directly to residential or commercial buildings. FTTB+LAN stands for Fiber-to-the-Building with Local Area Network, which involves deploying fiber optics to a building and connecting it to a LAN for internal networking. FTTB+DSL combines fiber optics with digital subscriber line technology for high-speed internet access in multi-dwelling units.</p></p>
