<p><p>Photochromic smart glass uses advanced technology to change its tint based on the amount of sunlight it is exposed to. There are two main types of photochromic smart glass on the market: CdO Glass and AgX Glass. CdO Glass, or Cadmium Oxide Glass, utilizes cadmium oxide as the photochromic material. On the other hand, AgX Glass, or Silver-based Glass, uses silver compounds for its photochromic properties. Both types of smart glass offer effective light control and energy efficiency in a variety of applications.</p></p>
