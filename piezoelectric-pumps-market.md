<p><p>Piezoelectric pumps are divided into two main types - Standard Piezoelectric Pumps and Large Capacity Piezoelectric Pumps. Standard Piezoelectric Pumps are suitable for applications where a lower flow rate is required, such as in medical devices or analytical instruments. On the other hand, Large Capacity Piezoelectric Pumps are designed to handle higher flow rates and are used in applications like fuel delivery systems or cooling systems in electronic devices. Both types of pumps utilize the piezoelectric effect to generate fluid flow efficiently.</p></p>
