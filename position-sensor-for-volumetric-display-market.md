<p><p>Position sensors play a crucial role in the Volumetric Display market, particularly in technologies like Digital Light Processing (DLP) and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS). In DLP technology, the position sensor helps in creating images by accurately positioning mirrors that reflect light to form the desired projection. On the other hand, in LCOS technology, the position sensor aids in aligning liquid crystals on a silicon substrate to manipulate light and create high-resolution images. Both technologies rely on precise position sensing for optimal performance in volumetric displays.</p></p>
