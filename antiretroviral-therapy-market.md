<p><ul><li>NNRTIs</li><li>NRTI</li><li>Multiclass Combination Products</li><li>Integrase Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antiretroviral Therapy market consists of various types of drugs used to treat HIV/AIDS. NNRTIs (Non-nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitors) block the reverse transcriptase enzyme, NRTIs (Nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitors) interfere with viral replication, Multiclass Combination Products combine different classes of drugs for increased effectiveness, and Integrase Inhibitors prevent integration of viral DNA into the host cell genome. These different types of drugs target different stages of the HIV life cycle, providing a comprehensive approach to managing the virus.</p></p>
