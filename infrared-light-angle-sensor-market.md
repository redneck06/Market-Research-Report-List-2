<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Infrared Light Angle Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR (SWIR)</li><li>Mid Wave IR (MWIR)</li><li>Long Wave (LWIR)</li><li>Far Wave (FWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared light angle sensors are categorized based on the wavelength range they operate within. Short Wave IR (SWIR) sensors detect light in the range of 0.7 to 1.5 micrometers, while Mid Wave IR (MWIR) sensors detect light in the range of 3 to 5 micrometers. Long Wave (LWIR) sensors operate within the range of 8 to 12 micrometers, and Far Wave (FWIR) sensors detect wavelengths beyond 14 micrometers. Each type of sensor is suited for different applications based on the specific wavelength range they detect.</p></p>
