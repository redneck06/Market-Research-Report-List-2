<p><p>Rock face drill rigs can be categorized into three main types based on the number of booms they have: Single Boom Drilling Rigs, Double Boom Drilling Rigs, and Multi Boom Drilling Rigs. Single boom rigs are typically more compact and mobile, suitable for smaller drilling projects. Double boom rigs offer increased efficiency and productivity by allowing two holes to be drilled simultaneously. Multi boom rigs are the largest and most versatile, capable of drilling multiple holes at different angles and depths simultaneously, making them ideal for large-scale mining operations.</p></p>
