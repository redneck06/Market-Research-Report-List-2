<p><p>Organic water soluble fertilizers come in a variety of types such as meat residue, animal excreta, human excreta, vegetable compost, crop residue, and others. Meat residue fertilizers are derived from leftover animal products, while animal excreta fertilizers come from animal waste. Human excreta fertilizers are made from human waste, and vegetable compost fertilizers are created from decomposed plant matter. Crop residue fertilizers utilize leftover plant material from crops. Other types of organic water soluble fertilizers may include various natural substances and byproducts.</p></p>
