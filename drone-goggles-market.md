<p><p>Drone goggles come in two main types: box goggles and low profile goggles. Box goggles are typically bulkier and offer a more immersive viewing experience, resembling a virtual reality headset. On the other hand, low profile goggles are more lightweight and compact, perfect for users who prioritize portability and comfort. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of drone pilots, with box goggles offering a more intense experience and low profile goggles focusing on convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
