<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IoT RFID Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li><li>Semi-Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive RFID antennas do not have their own power source and rely on the RFID reader for power. Active RFID antennas have their own power source and can transmit signals over longer distances. Semi-Active RFID antennas also have their own power source but rely on the RFID reader for some functions. Each type of RFID antenna serves different purposes in IoT applications, with passive antennas being more cost-effective for short-range applications, active antennas being ideal for long-range tracking, and semi-active antennas offering a balance between the two.</p></p>
