<p><p>Thermal Bonding Film (TBF) Market Types include Thermoplastic TBF, TPU Film, PI Thermal Mucosa, and Thermoset TBF. Thermoplastic TBF is a type of TBF that softens when heated and hardens when cooled, providing a strong bond. TPU Film is a thermoplastic polyurethane film with high elasticity and durability. PI Thermal Mucosa is a type of TBF made from polyimide resin, known for its high temperature resistance. Thermoset TBF is a type of TBF that undergoes irreversible chemical bonding when heated, creating a strong and durable bond.</p></p>
