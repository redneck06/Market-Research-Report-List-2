<p><p>Intrusion Detection & Prevention Systems (IDPS) are security solutions designed to detect and prevent unauthorized access to computer networks. IDPS can be categorized into two main types: hardware-based and software-based systems. Hardware IDPS consist of physical devices that monitor network traffic and detect threats in real-time. On the other hand, software IDPS are software applications that run on servers or workstations, providing similar functions as hardware-based systems but with the flexibility of being installed on existing infrastructure. Both hardware and software IDPS play a crucial role in protecting networks from cyber threats.</p></p>
