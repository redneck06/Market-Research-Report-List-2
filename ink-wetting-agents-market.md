<p><p>Ink wetting agents are chemicals that help improve the wetting ability of ink on different surfaces. There are two main types of ink wetting agents: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based ink wetting agents are environmentally friendly and are mainly used in water-based inks. On the other hand, solvent-based ink wetting agents are more commonly used in solvent-based inks. Solvent-based agents are effective in improving ink flow and adhesion on non-porous surfaces, while water-based agents are preferred for their non-toxic and sustainable properties.</p></p>
