<p><ul><li>Single Face Brake</li><li>Power Off Brake</li><li>Particle Brake</li><li>Hysteresis Power Brake</li><li>Multiple Disk Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EM brake market consists of various types including Single Face Brake, Power Off Brake, Particle Brake, Hysteresis Power Brake, and Multiple Disk Brake. Single Face Brake operates by applying pressure to a rotating shaft. Power Off Brake stops rotation when power is turned off. Particle Brake uses magnetic particles to create friction. Hysteresis Power Brake uses magnetic hysteresis to generate resistance. Multiple Disk Brake has multiple disks stacked together to increase braking power. Each type offers unique features and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
