<p><p>ROADM WSS components are critical in enabling dynamic reconfiguration of optical networks. The market types for these components include Blocker-Based, PLC-Based, and Wavelength Selective Switches (WSS). Blocker-Based technology uses fixed filters to block unwanted wavelengths. PLC-Based devices use planar lightwave circuits for filtering. Wavelength Selective Switches offer more advanced functionality with the ability to route specific wavelengths to different ports. The Edge market focuses on the deployment of ROADM WSS components at the network edge for efficient and flexible optical networking solutions.</p></p>
