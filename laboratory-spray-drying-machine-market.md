<p><p>Laboratory spray drying machines are classified into different types based on the method of drying. Pressure spray dryers use high pressure to atomize the feed liquid into droplets. Stream spray dryers use hot gas to evaporate the solvent in the feed liquid. Centrifuge spray dryers use centrifugal force to atomize the feed liquid. Each type has its own advantages and applications in laboratory settings, catering to different drying requirements and preferences.</p></p>
