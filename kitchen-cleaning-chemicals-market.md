<p><strong>The Kitchen Cleaning Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkali Cleaning Chemicals</li><li>Strong Acid Cleaning Chemicals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen cleaning chemicals market can be segmented into two main types- alkali cleaning chemicals and strong acid cleaning chemicals. Alkali cleaning chemicals are substances with a high pH level that are effective in removing grease, oil, and proteins from kitchen surfaces. On the other hand, strong acid cleaning chemicals have a low pH level and are used to dissolve mineral deposits, such as limescale, from kitchen equipment and utensils. Both types of chemicals play a crucial role in maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in commercial kitchens.</p></p>
