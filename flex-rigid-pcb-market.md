<p><p>Flex Rigid PCB, also known as flexible-rigid printed circuit boards, are a combination of flexible and rigid PCBs connected with flexible connectors. Flex Rigid PCBs are commonly used in applications where space is limited and multiple board-to-board connections are required.</p><p>The Flex Rigid PCB Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.30% during the forecast period. <p><p>Flex rigid PCBs come in various types such as single layer, double layer, and multi-layer. Single layer flex rigid PCBs have a single layer of conductive material, providing a simple and cost-effective solution. Double layer flex rigid PCBs have two layers of conductive material, allowing for more complex designs and tighter connections. Multi-layer flex rigid PCBs have three or more layers, offering even greater design flexibility and space savings. Each type caters to different requirements and specifications, making them suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
