<p><p>Bulletproof helmets are available in three main types based on the material used: metal, nonmetal, and composite. Metal helmets are typically made of steel or titanium, offering high levels of protection but can be heavy and bulky. Nonmetal helmets are usually constructed from materials like Kevlar or polyethylene, providing good protection with lighter weight. Composite helmets combine materials like ceramic and aramid fibers to offer a balance between protection, weight, and comfort. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different needs and budgets.</p></p>
